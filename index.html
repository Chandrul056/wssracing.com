About WSSR

What WSSR is

WSSR is a premium sim-racing competition platform and operating system built to organize, standardize, and elevate racing experiences across studios, communities, and independent drivers.

🏁

Structured championships

Multi-round seasons with professional rules, standings, operations, and stewarding.

🎮

Flexible participation

Drivers can compete from home rigs or partner studios depending on the competition format.

⚡

Format agnostic

WSSR can operate offline, online, or hybrid competitions based on what works best for the partner and event.

Ecosystem

How the WSSR ecosystem works

Studios provide infrastructure, communities bring engagement, drivers participate, and WSSR connects everything through structured competition.

01

Studios and communities join

Partner studios host events and support drivers while communities bring racers, identity, and participation.

02

WSSR builds the competition system

WSSR defines the format, rules, standings, stewarding, and race operations for a professional racing experience.

03

Drivers compete from anywhere

Drivers race from their home rigs or from any partner studio, depending on what access they have.

Who WSSR serves

  • Studios — improve rig utilization and event quality.
  • Communities — bring more structure and credibility.
  • Drivers — gain access to serious competition.

Why this model matters

  • Sim racing is growing, but competition is fragmented.
  • WSSR adds structure, fairness, and repeatability.
  • It is designed as a win-win ecosystem, not a competing community.
